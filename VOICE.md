# VOICE.md, or: how this place talks

These rules bind every word a player ever reads: buttons, toasts, missions, README,
error messages, commit messages. Anyone building on this repo, human or AI, reads
this first and writes accordingly. Same discipline as Quietly's design rules:
tokens for color, laws for language.

## The laws

1. **Zero jargon, tech or gaming.** Words like "sync", "submit", "configure", "XP",
   "level up", "respawn" belong to products and consoles. This place says the human
   thing: "bottle our progress", "throw the gauntlet", "the house always pays".
   It should FEEL like a game while sounding like a person.

2. **Zero AI fluff.** Banned on sight: "unlock your potential", "embark on a journey",
   "dive into", "seamlessly", "empower", "elevate", "delve". If a sentence could
   caption a LinkedIn post, kill it and write what a funny friend would say.

3. **Human, witty, sarcastic, playful.** Dry sarcasm, poker face, best-friend
   teasing. Warmth under every roast, kindness underneath everything, and each
   line sounds like a sharp friend talking.

4. **Zero em dashes.** Commas, periods, or a fresh sentence. An em dash is a tell.

5. **Zero negation framing.** Say what happens, skip what forbids. "Eyes on your own
   work" beats "no peeking". "Takebacks expired in 1997" beats "no takebacks".
   Rules land harder when they arrive as jokes.

## Calibration examples

| Never | Always |
|-------|--------|
| Submit your solution | Done. Pay me. |
| Data synchronized successfully | Poured back in. No paradoxes detected. |
| Invalid input | A gauntlet needs a name. |
| Complete challenges to earn points | Losers explain. Winners git push. |
| An error occurred | GitHub said no. Touch grass for sixty seconds and retry. |
| Update your profile avatar | Pick your fighter. It says everything about you. |

## Where wit is NOT allowed to blur meaning

Instructions must still work. A joke that leaves the player unsure what to *do*
is a failed joke. Every witty line either stands next to a working command,
an exact path, or a clear next step — or it gets cut.

## Tone boundaries

- Roasts target effort, streaks, and code. Never bodies, never character, never anything
  a player can't fix by coding more.
- Platonic always. Competitive warmth, sibling-level teasing, nothing further.
- Losses are funny, debts are funny, the ledger is funny. Payment is always honored.
